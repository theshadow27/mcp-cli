## Patterns established

- **Workers are conversations, not batch jobs.** The orchestrator can and should interact with spawned sessions via `mcx claude send`. When a worker asks for plan approval, answer it. When a worker goes off track, redirect it. Bye-and-respawn is the last resort, not the first. Updated `run.md` with "Interacting with workers" section.
- **5-minute poll intervals** are the new default. 30-second was too chatty, 5-minute is the right balance for sprint orchestration.
- **Already-fixed issues are cheap** — sessions detect and close them in <$0.30 and <2 minutes. Including them in sprint plans is fine; they free slots quickly.
- **Triage caught a catastrophic PR** — PR #1199 (125k deletions) was flagged by triage metrics before QA could merge it. This safety net must be preserved when migrating to event-driven work item tracking.
- **Investigate before respawning** — every "weird" event this sprint had a clear cause when investigated: plan approval waiting, routine permission routing, dirty worktree. The pattern of waving off issues and moving on wastes money and hides problems.
