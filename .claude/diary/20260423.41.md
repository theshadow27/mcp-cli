## Patterns established

- **Admin-merge is routine during sprints** until #1621 is resolved. Don't burn time trying to coax branch protection past the cancelled-runs state; the checks are actually green, just confused. Document the rationale in the merge commit or leave it implicit (merge happens right after latest run = SUCCESS).
- **QA failing on unreplied Copilot threads is the norm, not the exception.** Every PR this sprint bounced at least once on "N unaddressed Copilot inline comments."
