## Patterns established

- **Update-branch on merge-readiness, not preemptively**, when a mid-sprint merge (here #2112) fixes a flake every other branch carries. Clears the cascade without N² CI.
- **Always adversarial-review a flaky fix even when the diff is a 1-liner** — triage routed #2112's fix to qa (low churn); overrode to review per `investigations.md` step 5 to confirm the mechanism. Worth keeping as the explicit override.
- **Repair spawns should `git merge origin/main` first** when a flake fix has landed mid-sprint, so re-QA doesn't re-flake.
