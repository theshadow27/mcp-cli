## Patterns established

- **Fresh-context opus for stuck-orchestrator diagnosis**: when a sprint drags >2 hours on a single issue, spawn a fresh opus session with "read all the comments and trajectories, report what the problem is and why we've been struggling" — semantic conflicts (like the #1513/#1515 test-vs-impl contradiction) are invisible to the orchestrator who's been in the weeds.
- **Workaround allergy**: "there's a dangerous sequence of manual toolcalls, so we have a workaround" is a smell, not a solution. If a bug recurs across 3 sprints (#1398) or blocks a sprint twice (#1506), promote to P0 and fix, don't document the workaround.
- **Opus-4.7-one-shot as the issue granularity primitive**: replaces story-point estimation for sub-issue filing. The orchestrator is a better judge of this than the human because it knows Opus's shape.
- **Dedup with detail-fold**: when closing as dup, append unique details from closed sibling to canonical issue body before closing. Makes the canonical strictly more useful; preserves the cluster's full surface.
- **Sprint target rebudgeting based on anchor risk**: split "N PRs" into "M scoped anchors + K filler backfill" when anchors are architectural (e.g. cross-thread bus). Prevents anchor slip from consuming the whole sprint.
